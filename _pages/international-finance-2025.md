## Syllabus

### First Part

**Introduction**
* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Ch. 1
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 1
* Melvin and Norbin (2022), Ch. 1-2

**Exchange Rates and the International Markets**
* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Ch. 3 and 4
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 5
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Ch. 2
* Burstein and Gopinath (2014). "International Prices and Exchange Rates," *Handbook of International Economics*, Volume 4, pp. 391-451
* Gopinath and Itskhoki (2022). "Dominant Currency Paradigm: a review," *Handbook of International Economics*, Volume 6, pp. 45-90
* Lustig, Roussanov, and Verdelhan (2011). "Common risk factors in currency markets," *The Review of Financial Studies*, 24(11)
* Chaboud, Rime, and Sushko (2023). "The foreign exchange market," *Research Handbook of Financial Markets*, pp. 253-275

**Balance of Payments and the International Accounts**
* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Ch. 5
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 3

### Second Part

**International Parity and Forecasting the Exchange Rates**
* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Ch. 6
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 6

**Capital Flows and the International Assets**
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 15

### Third Part

**Options, Swaps, and Hedging**
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 9 and 10

**Macroeconomic Policy, Coordination, and Macroprudential Policies**
* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Ch. 7 and 9

**FDI and M&A**
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Ch. 16

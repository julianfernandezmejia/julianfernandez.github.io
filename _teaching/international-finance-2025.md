## Syllabus

Detailed weekly schedule and grading policy are shared on Campus Virtual.

## First Part

### Introduction

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapter 1.
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapter 1.
* Melvin and Norbin (2022), Chapters 1-2.

### Exchange Rates and International Markets

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapters 3-4.
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapter 5.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapter 2.
* Burstein and Gopinath (2014), [International Prices and Exchange Rates](https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-444-54314-1.00007-0), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 4.
* Gopinath and Itskhoki (2022), [Dominant Currency Paradigm: a review](https://doi.org/10.1016/bs.hesint.2022.02.009), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 6.
* Lustig, Roussanov, and Verdelhan (2011), [Common risk factors in currency markets](https://doi.org/10.1093/rfs/hhr068), Review of Financial Studies, 24(11).
* Chaboud, Rime, and Sushko (2023), [The foreign exchange market](https://www.e-elgar.com/shop/gbp/research-handbook-of-financial-markets-9781800375314.html), in Research Handbook of Financial Markets.

### Balance of Payments and International Accounts

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapter 2.
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapter 3.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapter 4.
* Gourinchas and Rey (2007), [International Financial Adjustment](https://doi.org/10.1086/521966), Journal of Political Economy, 115(4), 665-703.
* Gourinchas, Rey, and Sauzet (2019), [The International Monetary and Financial System](https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-economics-080217-053518), Annual Review of Economics, 11, 859-893.

## Second Part

### International Parity and Exchange Rate Forecasting

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapter 5.
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapter 6.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapters 6-9.
* Melvin and Norbin (2022), Chapters 6-8.
* Engel (2014), [Exchange Rates and Interest Parity](https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-444-54314-1.00008-2), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 4.
* Du and Schreger (2022), [CIP deviations, the dollar, and frictions in international capital markets](https://doi.org/10.1016/bs.hesint.2022.03.001), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 6.
* Hyndman and Athanasopoulos (2021), [Forecasting: Principles and Practice](https://otexts.com/fpp3/index.html), Chapters 3, 5, and 9.
* Cheung et al. (2019), [Exchange rate prediction redux](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jimonfin.2018.03.010), Journal of International Money and Finance, 95, 332-362.
* Engel and Wu (2023), [Forecasting the U.S. Dollar in the 21st Century](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jinteco.2023.103715), Journal of International Economics, 141, 103715.

### Capital Flows and International Assets

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapter 9.
* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapters 11-13 and 15.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapters 11-13.
* Forbes and Warnock (2012), [Capital Flow Waves: Surges, Stops, Flight, and Retrenchment](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jinteco.2012.03.006), Journal of International Economics, 88, 235-251.
* Maggiori, Neiman, and Schreger (2020), [International Currencies and Capital Allocation](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jinteco.2012.03.006), Journal of Political Economy, 128(6), 2019-2066.
* Coppola, Maggiori, Neiman, and Schreger (2021), [Redrawing the Map of Global Capital Flows](https://doi.org/10.1093/qje/qjab014), Quarterly Journal of Economics, 136(3), 1499-1556.
* Gourinchas and Rey (2014), [External Adjustment, Global Imbalances, Valuation Effects](https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-444-54314-1.00010-0), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 4.
* Miranda-Agrippino and Rey (2022), [The Global Financial Cycle](https://doi.org/10.1016/bs.hesint.2022.02.008), Handbook of International Economics, Vol. 6.

## Third Part

### Options, Swaps, and Hedging

* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapters 7-10.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapters 20-21.
* Melvin and Norbin (2022), Chapter 4.
* Ranaldo (2023), [Foreign exchange swaps and cross-currency swaps](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=4138134), in Research Handbook of Financial Markets.

### Macroeconomic Policy, Coordination, and Macroprudential Policies

* Krugman, Obstfeld, and Melitz (2022), Chapters 6-7.
* Feenstra and Taylor (2021), Chapters 3, 8-9.
* Claessens (2015), [An Overview of Macroprudential Policy Tools](https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-financial-111914-041807), Annual Review of Financial Economics, 7, 397-422.
* Forbes (2019), [Macroprudential Policy: What We've Learned, Don't Know, and Need to Do](https://www.aeaweb.org/articles?id=10.1257/pandp.20191056), AEA Papers and Proceedings, 109, 470-475.
* Forbes (2021), [The International Aspects of Macroprudential Policy](https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-economics-081020-051248), Annual Review of Economics, 13, 203-228.
* Blanchard (2017), [Currency Wars, Coordination, and Capital Controls](https://www.ijcb.org/journal/ijcb17q2a8.htm), International Journal of Central Banking, 13(2), 283-308.

### FDI and M&A

* Eun, Resnick, and Chuluun (2024), Chapter 16.
* Bekaert and Hodrick (2017), Chapter 14.
* Melvin and Norbin (2022), Chapter 11.
